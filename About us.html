Our Story

A Team from LNCT, Bhopal started "Tip Of Music" because we had a problem to select a song according to our state of mind. That's why we started thinking about a platform where we can easily listen our song according to our emotional state.

We as a team got a first lead when we get to know about Tenserflow. And this is the first step towards our solution. Then we decided to go through the WEB APPLICATION to exhibit our solution. That's how this "Tip Of Music" is here for us, yours and everyone.
